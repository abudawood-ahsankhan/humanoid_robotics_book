About This Curriculum

This comprehensive curriculum covers the full stack of humanoid robot development:

  • Foundation: ROS 2 concepts, node development, and simulation
  • Perception: Advanced computer vision and sensor integration
  • Cognition: AI planning and decision making with LLMs
  • Action: Control systems and physical execution
  • Integration: Complete Vision-Language-Action systems

All modules follow a spec-driven development approach ensuring clear requirements and systematic implementation.
